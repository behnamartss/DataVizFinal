Who are the top 10, 20 and 50 top singers with the most number of sales?

Shoreline

The goal is to show some visualization on the dataset of best selling singers all around the world. We will show some comparisons on different aspects of the dataset.

Task1

We want to show the 10, 20 and 50 top singers with the most number of sales.

Task2

We will have a comparison between the number of sales and TCU for 10,20,50 top singers with the most number of sales via multiple small charts. We want to see that do the singers with the highest numbers of sales have higher number of TCU too or not. TCU is total certified units which can be also comprised of units certified for other singers featured with the corresponding singer. Certifications are given to a singer based on the number of sales,streams and etc.

Task3

Showing the top 10,20,50 seller singers on a bubble chart with a color determined for each country. The x-axis would be the number of years active and the y-axis would be the number of sales. Hover shows the details of active years and sales with the name of the singer. We want to see that whether having more years being active necessarily makes a singer have more number of sales.

Task4

To what extent the 5-top most used genres have been used by singers? It is plotted on a pie chart.

Task5

We will plot a Waffle Chart for five top genres in terms of sales among all the signers' background comprised in the dataset to show signers born in which countries mostly tend to use that music genre in their work. This can show us the music taste of both artists and audiences which constitues the highest number of sales for a genre

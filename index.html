Data Visualization Project

Get Started

In this project we are going to manipulate a dataset called best selling artists ever which is provided as a csv file. The last of column named years_active is one of the columns I added using python to have a more completed dataset. This dataset is an unstructured dataset which means we can visualize two meaningless charts at most using just those columns. That is to say, we need many manipulations and text analysis to make that data usable in a way we want. This dataset includes ranges of numbers in a string format or also different important data all in string format next to each other without any specific pattern which makes the dataset challenging to work with. The following list of best-selling music artists includes those music acts from the 20th century to the present with claims of 75 million or more record sales worldwide.

What are we going to see?

The goal is to show some visualization on the dataset of best selling singers all around the world. We will show some comparisons on different aspects of the dataset.

Task1

We want to show the 10, 20 and 50 top singers with the most number of sales.

Task2

We will have a comparison between the number of sales and TCU for 10,20,50 top singers with the most number of sales via multiple small charts. We want to see that do the singers with the highest numbers of sales have higher number of TCU too or not. TCU is total certified units which can be also comprised of units certified for other singers featured with the corresponding singer. Certifications are given to a singer based on the number of sales,streams and etc.

Task3

Showing the top 10,20,50 seller singers on a bubble chart with a color determined for each country. The x-axis would be the number of years active and the y-axis would be the number of sales. Hover shows the details of active years and sales with the name of the singer. We want to see that whether having more years being active necessarily makes a singer have more number of sales.

Task4

To what extent the 5-top most used genres have been used by singers? It is plotted on a pie chart.

Task5

We will plot a Waffle Chart for five top genres in terms of sales among all the singers' background comprised in the dataset to show singers born in which countries mostly tend to use that music genre in their work. This can show us the music taste of both artists and audiences which constitues the highest number of sales for a genre

A comparison between the number of Sales and TCU . Do they differ to a high extent?

One of the concepts in music industry is the TCU phenomenon. TCU is an abbreviation for Total Certified Units. This concept has emerged in the USA. it includes the number of streams and the number of plays on different platforms. Anyway, The dataset does not give a specific information on that and it seems it can have other parameters too. By the way, this specifies some certifications given by a relevant governing body. These certifications are given when a certain number of units have been sold, streamed, or downloaded,and they serve as a measure of an artist's commercial success. As a matter of fact, Anyway, it is better to have some comparisons between each artist's sales and TCU to see does necessarily an artist with higher sales, have higher TCU too or not. According to my findings, If a singer or a band has more number of sales, does not mean it has the highest number of TCUs too. Although Drake is 20th of the list in terms of sales, it has the highest TCU. On the other hand, The Beatles who has beaten a record in terms of sales, has a high number of TCU but not more than Drake and some other artists who have lower rankings in terms of sales. The other interesting result is that there are some major differenes in TCU among artists with the same number of sales . A good example would be Rihanna and Pink Floyed. Rihanna with 335 number of TCUs has much higher TCU than Pink Floyed. Eminem, Rihanna and Drake have the top TCUs, which means they have more commercial success. As a conclusion we can say that TCU and the number of sales have a positive correlation and are usually close,but we cannot say that the higher the number of sales is, the TCU is higher too.
